Bachelor in Applied ComputingrTechnology

The BCT is a four-year degree program thath focuses on the practical aspects of the science of computing.
The Program develops core skills in multimedia technology, web/mobile development and network/systems administration.
Furthermore, it prepares learners with supervisory and management skills.
The curriculum is derived fromt he BS Industrial Technology major in Computer Technology with CHED Certificate of Program Compliance (COPC) No. 31, series of 2021.

Program Objectives:

The Program prepare learners with the
practical skills in multimedia technology,
web mobile develoment and
network systems administration and
supervisorial skills to manage an organization
in this areas of competrencies, engage in a
startup business or pursue a teaching career.

Specific Objectives:

The learner should be able to:

1. Demonstate understanding of the importance of being an ethical computing
professional when making work-related decisions
2. Develop products or processes by applying their knowledge in computer or network
systems, and development of productivity tools.
3. Participate as an active member of a development/service team with leadership roles when appropriate
4. Apply communication skills effectively to promote ideas, goals, processes or products.
5. Make positive contributions to the community with the skills and abilities learned in the Program.
6. Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and
field-based opportunities.
